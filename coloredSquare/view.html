Hi, Greetings I am Amardeep Singh. This is my Coloring Board and this is how you use it.

  1. Left and right side here represent the Colors and Canvas respectively and both are self scrollable panes.
  2. Select a color by clicking on it and then clicking on the canvas to paint the particular square.
  3. Click Add color to add new colors and refresh color to to get new set of Random colors.
  4. Use the '+' and '-' buttons to increase and decrease the size of the canvas.
  5. Enjoy!! See you soon.
  6. Click any where on the screen to get to the Board.
